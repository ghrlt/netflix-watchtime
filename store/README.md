# Store assets

Everything for the Chrome Web Store listing of Netflix Watchtime.

- **[listing.md](listing.md)** — copy/paste text for the developer dashboard: summary,
  detailed description, single-purpose statement, per-permission justifications, data-usage
  disclosures, and the privacy-policy URL.

- **screenshots/** — 5 × 1280×800 PNG (suggested upload order; the first is shown in search):
  1. `1-hero.png` — total watch time + stat cards
  2. `2-charts.png` — movies vs episodes · when you watched
  3. `3-when-you-watch.png` — by hour of day · day of week · top series
  4. `4-leaderboard-optin.png` — the in-extension leaderboard opt-in
  5. `5-public-leaderboard.png` — the public board at netflixwatchtime.zlef.fr

- **promo/**
  - `promo-tile-440x280.png` — small promo tile
  - `promo-marquee-1400x560.png` — marquee promo

Screenshots use **synthetic demo data**, not real viewing history. The privacy policy is
hosted at <https://netflixwatchtime.zlef.fr/privacy>.
